ABOUT US

NoQ HELPS IN REDUCING THE RISK OF INFECTION BOTH IN STORES AND IN QUEUES OUTSIDE BY USING VIRTUAL QUEUING SYSTEM

HOW IT WORKS

Shopkeepers can set duration for their shop for next day.

Customers can book a slot in available shops as per their convenience for the same day or next day.

After booking a slot, the customer will receive an auto-generated message with an OTP regarding slot booked.

Customers need to arrive at the shop and show the message to the shopkeeper before entering the shop.

Shopkeepers can view details of customers who have booked a slot in their shop.
